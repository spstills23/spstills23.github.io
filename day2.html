Iceland Adventure Day Two

Day 2 starts from Reykjavik and continues till we hit the small town of Vik. Along the way we visit the famous waterfall, Seljalandsfoss, known for its great height and hidden cave behind it. After we took a stop there we continued on to the next "foss" where we were able to walk right up to the base of it and witness the incredible rainbow made by it's mist. Next on our lists were the Glacier Lagoon, geothermal hotsprings, blacksand beaches and basalt caves. To cap the day off we got a great glymps of the Northern Lights!
